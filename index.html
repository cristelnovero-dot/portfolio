Artist Statement

Art, for me, is a way to express ideas and feelings without using words.

As a student, I am learning how to combine technology and creativity through digital art. This portfolio shows my work from eleven modules, where I explored different styles and techniques.

My projects include collage, drawing, abstract design, infographic, and meme creation. Each activity helped me improve my skills and understand how to communicate visually. I am also inspired by Filipino culture, especially its colors, patterns, and stories, which I included in some of my works.

Through these projects, I learned not only how to create art but also why art is important. This portfolio shows my growth and learning as a student and artist.

— Cristel Joy Novero, 2025–2026
